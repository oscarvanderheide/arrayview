# Display

## Colormaps

`c` cycles through defaults: gray, lipari, navia, viridis, plasma, and more.
`C` enters any matplotlib colormap by name.

## Dynamic range

`d` cycles quantile presets: 0–100%, 1–99%, 5–95%, 10–90%. Also expands the colorbar into a full histogram with KDE curve.
`D` sets vmin/vmax manually — locked until next `d`.

## Window / level

Drag the colorbar to shift the window. Scroll on the colorbar to narrow or widen the range. Double-click to reset.

## Log scale

`L` toggles logarithmic display.

## Themes

`T` cycles: dark (default), light, solarized, nord.

## Masking

`M` cycles Otsu threshold levels (8 steps). Useful for isolating structures.

## Layout

`b` toggles a border around the canvas. `a` stretches panes to a square aspect ratio.
